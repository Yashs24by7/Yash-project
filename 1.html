Digital crafts* is the use of digital tools and technologies in traditional craft activities. This can include using computer-aided design (CAD) software to create patterns for quilting or knitting, or using 3D printing to create custom jewelry or craft supplies. [Image of Digital craft - Embroidery] [Image of Digital craft - 3D printed jewelry] * *Digital crafting* is the creation of digital assets, such as illustrations, animations, or 3D models, that can be used in a variety of creative projects. Digital crafting can be done using a variety of software programs, such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, or Blender. * *Digital art and craft studios* are businesses that provide digital tools and technologies for use in craft activities. These studios may offer classes on using digital tools for crafting, or they may provide access to equipment such as 3D printers or laser cutters.
